About

One tomorrow asks people everywhere to protect the future we share.

OneTomorrow is a public commitment to dignity, truth, shared responsibility, and peaceful action. It is simple enough for a child to understand and serious enough for communities, institutions, and nations to practice.

Why it matters Make the commitment

What OneTomorrow means

OneTomorrow means the future is not owned by one country, party, class, religion, or generation. Every community has a role in protecting public trust, human dignity, and the living world we all depend on.

Who belongs

Young people, elders, parents, workers, teachers, builders, caregivers, artists, people of faith, people of no faith, migrants, neighbors, and leaders can all belong here when they choose responsibility over cynicism.

Dignity
No person is disposable, invisible, or beneath public concern.
Public trust
Power, money, and decisions can be questioned without fear.
Commitment
A steady choice to act with integrity, especially when progress is slow.
