Global corruption

Corruption remains a global brake on trust.

Transparency International's 2025 index reports a global average of 42, with more than two-thirds of countries scoring below the midpoint.

Source: Transparency International
Global improvement

Progress is real, but uneven.

The UN's 2025 SDG reporting says only 35 percent of assessed targets are on track or showing moderate progress, making competent public delivery urgent.

Source: United Nations SDG Report
Global innovation

Innovation is accelerating and concentrating.

WIPO's 2025 findings describe innovation at a crossroads: AI and quantum technologies advance while innovation finance and access face pressure.

Source: WIPO Global Innovation Index
Clean energy

Renewables keep changing the economics of the future.

IRENA's 2026 update reports that renewable capacity reached 5,149 GW after a 692 GW surge in 2025, with renewables dominating new capacity additions.

Source: IRENA
Public integrity

Disclosure is becoming a democratic infrastructure issue.

Budgets, contracts, lobbying, conflicts, and assets are no longer paperwork. They are the public sensors that help societies detect capture early.
