Your part matters

You do not need permission to begin making trust visible.

The commitment

  • I will reject corrupt bargains that trade freedom, fairness, truth, or the future for comfort.
  • I will defend transparent institutions, equal justice, open opportunity, and broad prosperity.
  • I will measure strength by service: protecting the vulnerable, rewarding excellence, guarding the planet, and securing a civic floor beneath every person.
  • I will build upward with people of good faith across culture, class, party, religion, age, and place.
