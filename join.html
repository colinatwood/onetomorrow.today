Joining means practicing dignity, honesty, accountability, and shared responsibility in your own language, place, culture, and form of service.

The commitment

  • I will treat every person as worthy of dignity, even during disagreement.
  • I will seek truth, share information responsibly, and correct mistakes openly.
  • I will oppose corruption, cruelty, and abuse without becoming cruel myself.
  • I will connect local service to global responsibility for people and the planet.
